    Caturus: S**t man, this wizard war is f**ked. I just saw an elf dive into the Northern Wyrm and say “don’t be afraid” or some similar s**t, and then the Wyrm turned inside out, had her body explode, and then disappeared. The camera didn’t even go onto her, that’s how common s**t like this is.

    Yep, I knew Red’s drawing hand would be in danger this page, and what pretty, gory, swirly danger it is. While the last panel is chaotic, the interconnected and complete swirls across the whole Wyrm shows this is a deliberate act by Alinua; the Wyrm’s previous life magic swirls were all broken and chaotic, implying the healing was not controlled by itself.
    The lack of SFX on the first two panels compared to the final SHRIP gives a sense of slowness to them; Alinua took the time to spread her magic throughout the Wyrm completely, THEN executed her spell in one stroke.

    I was 50/50 between Alinua communing with the spirit of the Northern Wyrm and following her will and directive to reformat her incarnation back to what she wanted, or disintegration. Guess the later won out.
    Mind you, if N.W. wanted to de-incarnate, then the complete dissolution of her current body IS what she would want Alinua to do. I suspected a few pages back that that want to de-incarnate was why Ali’s “feel it out” magic did what it did to her tail.

    Prediction for next week: Out of the dead flesh of the beast rises the green glow of Alinua, who is then flocked by the mer-peoples thanking her as she tries to swim towards Selach and Valia to apply the healing. Maybe next Friday’s page will feature the Northern Wyrm re-incarnating in a new, uncorrupted body, to personally thank her too.
